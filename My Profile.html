Get to know me...

Hi, my name is Deanne Isabel Perez, and I am a 20-year-old Education Student majoring in Social Studies at Tayabas Western Academy. Aside from my studies, I have a deep passion for singing and have joined many competitions in the past. Singing has always been an avenue for me to express myself, and I find it fulfilling to share my talent with others. When I'm not singing, I enjoy eating and trying out new foods. I also have a creative side and love making artistically designed instructional materials. In my free time, I love spending time with my furry friends, particularly dogs. As a Social Studies major, I am passionate about learning about the world and the people in it, and I hope to make a positive impact in my community one day.

My songs and my major...

Singing has always been my passion. Ever since I was young, I loved listening to music and singing along to my favorite tunes. As I grew older, I discovered my talent in singing and started joining various singing competitions. The feeling of being on stage and sharing my voice with others was exhilarating. Singing became my escape from reality, and it has been my stress reliever ever since. As I entered college, I knew that I wanted to pursue a career in education. I have always loved working with children and making a difference in their lives. That's why I took up Social Studies as my major. It's a perfect fit for me because I can combine my passion for teaching and my love for history and culture. I believe that by teaching Social Studies, I can help my students become more open-minded and culturally aware, which is crucial in today's society. Singing and teaching are two different things, but they both bring me joy and fulfillment. I am grateful for the opportunity to pursue my passions and make a difference in the world.
